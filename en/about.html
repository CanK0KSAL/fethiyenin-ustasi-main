    Our Process from Discovery to Delivery

    1. Discovery & Needs Analysis

      On-site inspection, measurement, and technical condition assessment. Photo/video recording for current condition documentation.

    2. Written Quote & Scope

      Work items, measurement, material brand/model, and work schedule are written clearly.

    3. Sample & Approval

      Sample application and color/texture approval for silk/Italian plaster, paint, coatings, etc.

    4. Application & Control

      Daily progress report; intermediate inspection at critical stages (leveling, primer, insulation, etc.).

    5. Delivery & Warranty

      Completion report, usage/maintenance recommendations, and workmanship warranty.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What's included in the quote?

    Measurement, material brand/model, work schedule, delivery conditions, warranty, and payment plan are included in writing.

    What is the warranty period?

    Workmanship warranty of 12–24 months is provided depending on work scope. Manufacturer material warranties are also valid.

    How is the payment plan?

    Generally in the form of advance payment + progress payments. Amount and schedule are specified in the quote-contract.

    Do you provide cleaning and area protection?

    Yes. Floor-corner protection, dust barriers, and daily maintenance; detailed cleaning upon delivery is standard.

    Which areas do you work in?

    Our priority areas: Fethiye, Çalış, Ovacık, Göcek. Contact us for other areas.
