    Buying Guide

    Color & Texture Selection

    • View the same sample in daylight and artificial light.
    • Plan general tone + accent wall combination.
    • Test texture difference on substrates like PVC, plaster, concrete, old paint.

    Substrate Preparation

    • Cracks should be bridged with fiber/tape.
    • Appropriate primer/hydro-isolation is required in humid areas.
    • Surface should be opened before primer with dust-free sanding and vacuum cleaning.

    Application & Time

    • Drying times between Italian plaster layers should be maintained.
    • Seams in wall fabric should be planned perpendicular to light.
    • Attention should be paid to ambient temperature/humidity range in stone plaster.

    Warranty & Maintenance

    • Workmanship warranty and material brand/series list are provided in writing.
    • Cleaning/maintenance instructions (wipeability, chemical compatibility) are shared.
    • Quick response for local corrections within the first 12 months.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the basic difference between silk plaster and paint?
    Silk plaster offers texture/depth, is breathable and creates no seams; paint is smooth and a quick solution.
    Which coatings are suitable for humid areas?
    Stone plaster and some Italian plaster systems offer higher durability with appropriate primer/isolation.
    Do you do sample applications?
    Yes. After inspection, we apply a sample on a small area and clarify the color/texture decision together.
    Do you provide warranty?
    Workmanship warranty and material brand/series list are provided in writing.
