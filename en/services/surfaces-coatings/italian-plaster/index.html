    Frequently Asked Questions

    Where does Italian plaster give the best results?
    It gives the best results on accent walls, reception/welcome areas and display window backgrounds with its brightness and depth effect.
    Can it be used in wet volumes?
    Not recommended in places directly exposed to water such as inside showers. Can be used in adjacent areas with proper sealer.
    How is cleaning and maintenance?
    Gentle cleaning with pH-neutral products is recommended. Abrasive chemicals should be avoided. Sealer coat protects the surface.
    Can the gloss level be controlled?
    Yes. The matte‚Äìsatin‚Äìgloss range can be controlled with number of coats, polishing pressure and sealer type.
