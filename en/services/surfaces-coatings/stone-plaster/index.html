    Frequently Asked Questions

    How is its durability on exterior facades?
    Provides high durability against UV/rain cycles with appropriate primer and sealer. Weather/rain conditions should be checked during application.
    Is maintenance and cleaning difficult?
    Dirt retention decreases with sealer. Regular cleaning with low-pressure water and pH-neutral products is sufficient; abrasive chemicals are not recommended.
    What if cracks occur?
    Local repairs can be made for substrate-related cracks. Risky areas are bridged with fiber/tape during inspection.
    Can it be used in wet areas?
    Not recommended in areas such as inside shower/pool. Can be used in adjacent areas with sealer.
