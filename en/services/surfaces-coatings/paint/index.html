    Frequently Asked Questions

    How many coats does it take to change existing dark color to light?
    Primer + at least 2 coats; 3rd coat may be needed for some colors. Depends on color coverage and substrate.
    Does odor remain after painting?
    We prefer low VOC products; with proper ventilation, odor disappears quickly.
    When should exterior facade be painted?
    Should be applied on days without precipitation, with low wind and surface temperature above +8°C.
    What about furniture?
    All furniture/floors are protected; delivered with detailed cleaning after completion.
