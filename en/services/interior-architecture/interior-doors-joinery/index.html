    Measurement & Swing Direction

    How to Measure?

    • Width x Height (wall opening) and wall thickness; measurement from three points.
    • Final level after floor covering; threshold/carpet allowance.
    • Wall‚Äìframe parallelism check with plumb‚Äìlevel.

    Right / Left Swing

    • As the person pushing the door: **if hinges are on the right: right swing**, if on the left: left swing.
    • Direction that prevents collision risk according to furniture and main circulation.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the advantage of hidden hinge?

    Provides minimal appearance, 3D adjustment capability and quiet operation.

    Why is magnetic lock preferred?

    Offers quiet and vibration-free closing compared to metal-tongue locks.

    Your recommendation for doors opening to wet rooms?

    Laminate/veneer surface and stainless hardware; attention to threshold and seal detail.

    Can existing frame be covered?

    Conditional; if plumb and levels are suitable, covering/renovation can be done, otherwise new adjustable frame is recommended.

    Do you provide warranty?

    We provide workmanship warranty; hardware subject to manufacturer conditions.
