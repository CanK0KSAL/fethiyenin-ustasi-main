    Acoustic Principles

    Absorber & Diffuser Balance

    We establish a balance between absorbers (fabric panel/felt) and diffusers (slat/geometric panel) for speech clarity.

    • Absorptive surfaces on side walls.
    • Diffusion on rear/front wall for sense of space.

    Reflection Control

    In spaces dominated by glass/hard floors, first reflections are reduced to lower reverberation and echo sensation.

    • Acoustic solutions at ceiling‚Äìwall corner junctions.
    • Mixed strategy supported by furniture/carpet.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Does echo completely disappear with acoustic panels?

    Echo sensation significantly decreases; balanced absorber‚Äìdiffuser combinations are applied according to space and usage scenario.

    Can fabric panels be cleaned?

    Dusting is easy; surface can be renewed with snap-on fabric covering.

    Does LED light heat damage the panel?

    Heat management is ensured with correct driver and profile; maintenance access covers are planned.

    Workmanship warranty provided; lighting/equipment subject to manufacturer conditions.
