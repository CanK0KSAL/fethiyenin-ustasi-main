    Plan Types & Ergonomics

    L-Shape

    Compact space, continuous countertop

    • Work triangle is short and efficient.
    • Revolving basket recommended for corner modules.
    • Countertop work area increases.

    U-Shape

    Intensive storage, wide countertop

    • Two corner solutions needed (revolving/drawer).
    • Lower–upper modules on three sides.
    • Passage openings should be checked.

    Island & Parallel

    Social kitchen & prep area

    • Water/electrical plan for island; ventilation.
    • Two countertop arms efficient in parallel.
    • Walking corridor ≥ 100–120 cm.

    Materials & Surfaces

    Body (Frame)

    • MDF — high screw hold; ideal for lacquer/veneer.
    • Particle Board — practical for economical modules.
    • Compact Laminate — wet volume and intensive use.

    Door/Surface

    • Lacquer Paint — matte/satin/glossy; color freedom.
    • Natural Veneer — oak/walnut grain depth.
    • Laminate/Acrylic — scratch/UV resistance; modern surface.
    • Solid — natural texture; periodic maintenance may be required.

    Edge Banding & Moisture

    • 0.8–2 mm PVC/ABS banding; steam/impact resistance.
    • Sealing details around sink.

    Countertop & Splashback

    • Quartz, granite, solid, laminate countertop.
    • Under/over sink, drip tray and joint solutions.

    Mechanism & Accessories

    Rail & Hinge

    • Full-extension, soft-close drawer rails.
    • Clip-on hinge; corner/glass door options.

    Lift-Up & Sliding

    • Upward-opening mechanism (gas/lever).
    • Silent/soft-braking sliding door.

    Interior Layout

    • Fork–knife compartment drawer, pot organizer.
    • Hidden waste/recycling, pantry systems.

    Electrical & Lighting

    • LED profile/strip; sensor interior lighting.
    • Hidden outlet, device cable routing.

    Electrical & Utilities Checklist

    Electrical

    • Countertop outlet heights and count.
    • Appliance lines (refrigerator, oven, range hood).
