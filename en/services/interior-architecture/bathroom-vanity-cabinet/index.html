    Frequently Asked Questions

    Which surface is most suitable for wet rooms?

    Compact laminate and laminate/acrylic surfaces offer high durability; lacquer can be preferred with correct primer/varnish system.

    Wall-mounted or floor-standing?

    Wall-mounted is advantageous for cleaning and floor water; floor-standing solutions can be used according to storage needs.

    How to deal with fog and moisture?

    With proper ventilation, surface selection and lighting; fog-reducing solutions can also be considered in mirror cabinets.

    Do you provide warranty?

    Workmanship warranty provided; mechanisms and fixtures subject to manufacturer conditions.

    Maintenance recommendations?

    pH neutral products and microfiber cloth recommended; surfaces should be dried immediately after heavy water contact.
