    Paint Systems

    Acrylic

    Economical & Durable

    • Good UV resistance and weather protection.
    • Common choice for standard facades.
    • Proper primer and surface prep balance.

    Silicone

    High Vapor Permeability

    • Higher water repellency and vapor permeability.
    • Advantageous in humid and coastal areas.
    • Attention to surface preparation and primer compatibility.

    Elastomeric

    Flexible & Crack-Bridging

    • High flexibility and crack-bridging capability.
    • Preferred on surfaces with movement risks.
    • Correct film thickness and application technique required.

    Surface Preparation & Critical Details

    Typical Paint Process

    1. Surface inspection + peeling/crack removal.*
    2. Repair mortar application (localized or full coverage).
    3. Primer application (substrate-specific primer).
    4. First coat paint (even coverage, no runs).
    5. Final coat paint (smooth finish, color consistency).

    * According to manufacturer recommendations based on substrate type.

    Detail Discipline

    • Sills & Drip Edges: Continuous profile, sufficient slope.
    • Window Frames: Proper masking and edge treatment.
    • Joints/Expansion: Elastic fill, movement gaps.
    • Water Management: Rain downpipes and anchor transitions sealed.

    Durability & Performance

    Weather Resistance

    • UV resistance increases with L* value ‚Üí color retention.
    • Rain protection and water repellency minimize moisture ingress.
    • Color selection (dark tones) affects surface temperature.

    Substrate & Application

    • Proper primer ensures adhesion and prevents peeling.
    • Film thickness and layer count selected according to exposure.
    • Color consistency and coverage verified on exposed facades.

    Weather & Application Window

    Conditions

    • Application not performed during precipitation, strong wind and frost risk.
    • Shading and time spreading against direct sun/heat.
    • Surface moisture and dew point check mandatory.

    Site Safety

    • Scaffolding check and netting; precautions against fall risk.
    • Neighbor/ground protection; daily area handover plan.
    • Waste management and material storage organization.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Which paint system should I choose?

    According to location and goal: Acrylic (economy), Silicone (high vapor permeability), Elastomeric (flexibility). We decide together during inspection.

    My existing surface is peeling; how is it renewed?

    Weak layers cleaned, sound substrate established with repair mortar and primer; then layers applied.

    Is application done in precipitation?

    No. Application stopped if there is rain, strong wind and frost risk; curing times are observed.

    Is there warranty?

    We provide workmanship warranty; paint/coating subject to manufacturer warranty conditions.

    Will color fade occur?

    Risk reduced with UV resistant systems and correct tone selection; we observe technical limitations for dark tones.
