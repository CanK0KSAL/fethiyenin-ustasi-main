    Panel Selection

    EPS

    Economical & Efficient

    • Good thermal insulation, lightweight and easy to apply.
    • Common choice in standard residences.
    • Energy savings balance with correct thickness.

    XPS

    Low Water Absorption

    • Higher impact resistance and water repellency.
    • Advantageous in ground contact/ground-level areas.
    • Attention to joint and overlap discipline.

    Mineral Wool

    Fire & Sound

    • High fire resistance and sound insulation.
    • Preferred on wind-exposed floors and tall buildings.
    • Correct mesh render weight/anchoring count required.

    Layers & Critical Details

    Typical ETICS Layers

    1. Surface preparation + adhesive (frame + spots).*
    2. Panel layout (staggered joints) and expansion tracking.
    3. Mechanical anchoring (anchor suitable for wall type, sufficient count).
    4. Glass fiber mesh + corner/drip edge profiles.
    5. Thin render + primer + final coat paint/coating.

    * According to manufacturer recommendations based on wall type.

    Detail Discipline

    • Sills & Drip Edges: Continuous profile, sufficient slope.
    • Window Surrounds: Corner profile and mesh overlaps.
    • Joints/Expansion: Elastic fill, movement gaps.
    • Water Management: Rain downpipes and anchor transitions sealed.

    Thermal & Fire Performance

    Thermal Behavior

    • As thickness increases, U-value decreases ‚Üí fuel/energy savings.
    • Thermal bridges (columns/beams, balconies) minimized.
    • Color selection (dark tones) affects surface temperature.

    Fire & Wind

    • Mineral Wool: high fire resistance, recommended in multi-story buildings.
    • Anchoring and mesh render weight selected according to wind loads.
    • Anchorage and profile reinforcements on wind-exposed facades.

    Weather & Application Window

    Conditions

    • Application not performed during precipitation, strong wind and frost risk.
    • Shading and time spreading against direct sun/heat.
    • Surface moisture and dew point check mandatory.

    Site Safety

    • Scaffolding check and netting; precautions against fall risk.
    • Neighbor/ground protection; daily area handover plan.
    • Waste management and material storage organization.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Which panel should I choose?

    According to building location and goal: EPS (economy), XPS (low water absorption), Mineral Wool (fire/sound). We decide together during inspection.

    My existing surface is peeling; how is it renewed?

    Weak layers cleaned, sound substrate established with repair mortar and primer; then layers applied.

    Is application done in precipitation?

    No. Application stopped if there is rain, strong wind and frost risk; curing times are observed.

    Is there warranty?

    We provide workmanship warranty; paint/coating subject to manufacturer warranty conditions.

    Will color fade occur?

    Risk reduced with UV resistant systems and correct tone selection; we observe technical limitations for dark tones.
