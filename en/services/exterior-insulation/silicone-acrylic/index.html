    Silicone-Enhanced

    Stain repellent

    • Low water absorption, easy to clean.
    • Advantage in coastal/heavy rainfall areas.
    • Vapor permeable structure; blistering risk decreases.

    Acrylic

    Economical & balanced

    • Good adhesion, high color stability.
    • Wide color/texture range.
    • Solid system with proper primer.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Which coating should I choose: silicone or acrylic?

    In coastal/rainfall areas, silicone provides stain resistance and water repellency. Acrylic is an economical and balanced solution. We provide recommendations during inspection.

    What grain size should be preferred?

    1‚Äì1.5 mm modern and flat; 2 mm standard; 2.5 mm better hides impacts. Selected according to facade conditions.

    Is application done in rain?

    No. Application not performed if there is precipitation, strong wind and frost risk; curing times are waited.

    Can it be applied over sheathing?

    Yes. Silicone/acrylic coating can be applied after mesh render and proper primer.

    Will color fade occur?

    Risk reduced with UV resistant systems and correct tone selection; heating effect evaluated in dark tones.
