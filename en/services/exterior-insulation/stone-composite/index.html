    System & Material Options

    Natural Stone

    Travertine/Granite

    • High durability and prestigious appearance.
    • Stainless anchorage and secure joint configuration.
    • Long service life with ventilated gap.

    Porcelain/Gres

    Large format

    • Low water absorption and UV/stain resistance.
    • Hidden clip/channel system options.
    • Lightweight, easy-maintenance surface.

    Fiber-Cement

    Natural texture

    • Vapor permeable, impact resistant panels.
    • Paintable or ready-finish surface options.
    • Hidden/battened mounting alternatives.

    Composite Panel

    Aluminum Composite (ACM)

    • Lightweight and fast installation; wide color options.
    • Proper substructure and expansion configuration important.
    • Core selection suitable for environmental conditions.

    Substructure & Anchorage

    Support System

    • Stainless/Aluminum vertical-horizontal rails, adjustable brackets.
    • Anchor/chemical anchor selection according to wall type.
    • Thermal spacers that reduce thermal bridges.

    Ventilated Facade

    • Rear air gap; condensation and moisture management.
    • Entry-exit grilles and pest protection.

    Panel Mounting

    • Hidden clips, channels or adhesive + mechanical safety.
    • Point anchorage in stone and stainless connections.
    • Alignment: laser reference, staggering and modulation.

    Proper Substrate

    • Support wall check and flatness corrections.
    • Detail solution together with insulation/fire barriers.

    Joints & Critical Details

    Joint Management

    • Continuity of expansions and movement allowance.
    • Flexible joints with elastic sealant.
    • Modulation: window-door surround symmetry.

    Water Drainage

    • Drip edge and sill profiles, drip breakers.
    • Directed drainage in rear gap.

    Fire & Barriers

    • Fire barrier strips at floor levels.
    • Material/sub-system compatible detail components.
    • Zoning/discipline in ventilated gap.

    Detail Accessories

    • Corner profiles, drip edges and expansion covers.
    • Door/window reveals and finish battens.

    Performance & Safety

    Durability & Maintenance

    • Long service life against UV, rain and wind loads.
    • Low water absorption in stone/porcelain; stain resistance.
    • Easy maintenance on composite and fiber-cement surfaces.

    Safety Principles

    • Proper anchorage and panel safety elements.
    • Fire-stop barriers and rear gap control.
    • Correct modulation for wind load and expansion.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why is ventilated facade preferred?

    Rear gap manages moisture/condensation; helps surface stay long-lasting and clean.

    Natural stone or porcelain?

    Stone offers prestige and unique texture; porcelain has lightness, low water absorption and large format advantages.

    How is fire safety ensured?

    Safety principles applied with proper substructure, inter-floor fire barriers and zoning in rear gap.

    Does it require maintenance?

    Easy maintenance with periodic low-pressure washing and joint/seal checks.

    Can it be applied with insulation?

    Yes. Ventilated facade configurations over ETICS are possible; details solved together.
