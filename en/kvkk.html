    • Technical Data: IP address, cookie information, browser information

    • Analysis and improvement of website traffic

    4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

    Your personal data is processed based on the following legal grounds:

    • KVKK Art. 5/2 (c): "Processing of personal data made public by the data subject"
    • KVKK Art. 5/2 (e): "Processing is necessary for the establishment, exercise, or protection of a right"
    • KVKK Art. 5/2 (f): "Processing is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation"

    5. METHOD OF COLLECTING PERSONAL DATA

    Your personal data is collected through the following methods:

    • Contact forms filled out on the website
    • Requests sent via email
    • Telephone conversations
    • Face-to-face meetings
    • Cookies and similar technologies

    6. TRANSFER OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Your personal data may be transferred, within the framework of the conditions specified in Articles 8 and 9 of KVKK, and only when necessary for service provision, to the following groups in a limited manner:

    • Our service providers (hosting, email services)
    • Our business partners (companies providing technical services)
    • Authorized public institutions and organizations due to legal obligations

    7. RETENTION PERIOD OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Your personal data is retained for the period required by the processing purposes and in accordance with the limitation periods specified in the relevant legislation. When the processing purpose ends or the retention period expires, your personal data will be deleted, destroyed, or anonymized in accordance with KVKK.

    8. RIGHTS OF DATA SUBJECT

    In accordance with Article 11 of KVKK, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

    • To learn whether your personal data is being processed
    • To request information if it has been processed
    • To learn the purpose of processing and whether it is used appropriately
    • To know the third parties to whom your data is transferred domestically or abroad
    • To request correction if it is incomplete or inaccurate
    • To request deletion or destruction within the framework of the conditions set forth in KVKK
    • To request notification of third parties to whom your data has been transferred regarding correction, deletion, or destruction
    • To object to negative consequences arising from analysis of processed data exclusively through automated systems
    • To claim compensation for damages arising from unlawful processing

    9. APPLICATION METHOD

    To exercise your rights under KVKK, you can send an email to info@fethiyeninustasi.com with documents identifying your identity, make a written application, or contact us by phone at +90 506 022 29 00.

    Additionally, your legal rights against the decisions of the Personal Data Protection Board are reserved. You can submit your complaints to KVKK at www.kvkk.gov.tr.
