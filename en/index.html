    Process

    We proceed transparently, planned and dust-free from the first call to delivery.

    1. 1

      Needs & Goal Definition

      Free

      Through a short phone/WhatsApp conversation, we clarify scope, priorities, budget range and target date.

      Details
      • Usage purpose and expectations (durability, aesthetics, maintenance)
      • Estimated measurements, sharing existing plans/photos
      • Budget range and priority order (urgent/optional items)
      • Time target, access hours and living space constraints
      • Risk/obstacle predictions (moisture, electrical/gas lines, acoustic, etc.)
    2. 2

      On-Site Inspection & Measurement

      We take measurements at the address/from plans; we plan checks of current condition, risks and compliance (electrical, water, heating, acoustic, etc.).

      Details
      • Precise measurements with laser meter, detection of line and plumbing routes
      • Surface/floor integrity, straightedge and flatness check
      • Moisture/heat/acoustic condition (measurement when needed)
      • Infrastructure suitability: electrical, water, natural gas, drainage, flue
      • Preliminary review of regulation/compliance requirements (TS/EN, gas projects, etc.)
      • Photos + short inspection report
    3. 3

      Solution Design & Sample

      According to the nature of the work (plumbing/coating/carpentry/insulation), we recommend materials and systems; we provide samples/demo and technical drawings when needed.

      Details
      • System and material selection; technical data sheets
      • Work breakdown (item/m²/piece) and method to be applied
      • Color/texture catalog; on-site sample or mini demo option
      • Joints, corners, expansion and sealing details
      • Drawings/3D sketch or section when needed
      • Risks and Plan B (weather, supply, access, etc.)
    4. 4

      Clear Quote & Schedule

      We provide transparent quotes based on item/m²/piece; we determine contract, warranty terms and work schedule together.

      Details
      • Item-based pricing; material + labor separation
      • Included/excluded list (scaffolding, transport, waste, night work, etc.)
      • Payment plan, contract terms and warranty conditions
      • Start date, deadline and weekly work schedule
      • Color/material approval and order timing
    5. 5

      Preparation & Application

      We work without damaging the area with dust-free protection and workplace safety measures; we perform professional application according to approved methods.

      Details
      • Dust-free protection: floor/wall coverings, tape, zip doors
      • Workplace safety: electrical/gas safety, lockout-tagout (LOTO) requirements
      • Surface/floor preparation: scraping, repair, primer and appropriate system
      • Interruption plan (water/gas/electrical) and temporary solutions
      • Application with professional equipment and techniques
      • Daily progress report and photo sharing
    6. 6

      Inspection, Delivery & Documents

      We deliver with a checklist; we share maintenance guide, warranty document and compliance/test reports when needed.

      Details
      • Checklist tour with employer and acceptance certificate
      • Closing punch list (deficiencies/adjustments)
      • Cleaning and licensed waste disposal
      • Sharing maintenance & usage guide
      • Warranty document and invoice
      • Test/compliance reports when needed (pressure, leakage, thermal, etc.)
