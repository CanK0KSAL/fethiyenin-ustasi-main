    Fethiye-Specific Conditions & Solution Recommendations

    Climate & Facade

    • UV and temperature: UV-resistant paint/coating against color fading on south and west facades.
    • Humidity & salinity: Silicone-reinforced coatings against coastal effects; vapor-permeable system.
    • Wind & precipitation: Sill/drip profiles; watertight joints and ventilated facade construction.

    Building Types

    • Villa & detached house: Terrace/eaves and joint dilation are critical.
    • Apartment/condominium: Management coordination for common areas and facade decisions.
    • Business (hotel/cafe): Time-limited renovation, night work and area protection plan.

    Materials & Systems

    • Interior humidity-resistant surfaces; water resistance in bathroom/kitchen.
    • In carpentry, easy-to-maintain surfaces such as PVC coating/laminated/compact laminate.
    • Low-leak risk utilities in infrastructure; correct trap/line slope for odor and backflow.

    Planning

    • Seasonal effect: High demand in summer; inspection and material reservation done early.
    • Noise/site rules: Work hours compliant with condominium and municipality restrictions.
    • Maintenance plan: Annual inspection and cleaning recommendations after delivery.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can paint/coating be done in summer heat in Fethiye?

    It is done with shading and surface temperature control during peak sun hours; we plan morning/evening applications when needed.

    Which systems are suitable for humidity and salinity?

    Silicone-reinforced coatings, vapor-permeable paints and ventilated facade constructions provide resistance to coastal effects.

    Can you work with site/hotel work hour restrictions?

    Yes. We proceed with quiet equipment, dust control and specified time intervals in accordance with management rules.

    How do we clarify material selection?

    We do sample panels and trials in small areas, and decide together on color/texture and maintenance criteria.

    Do you provide warranty?

    We offer workmanship warranty. Maintenance recommendations and warranty documents are provided to you upon delivery.

    How does the inspection and quote process work?

    After inspection appointment, area and risk analysis, a clear quote is shared with written scope and schedule.
