    Ovacık-Specific Conditions & Solution Recommendations

    Climate & Elevation Impact

    • Wind load: High adhesion and joint solutions in anchorage and facade coating details.
    • UV and temperature difference: Coatings with high UV resistance against fading on south facade; expansion-tolerant materials inside.
    • Heavy precipitation/terraces: Drip, sill and joint water drainage, slope control.

    Architecture & Usage

    • Water insulation priority in joinery junctions on views-oriented facade.
    • Non-slip and non-warping surfaces in sun around pool/terrace.
    • Acoustic and privacy details on adjacent walls in villa/twin villa types.

    Materials & Systems

    • Thermal insulation with mineral wool or high-density EPS; silicone/silicate coating.
    • UV-resistant paint, silk/Italian plaster on interior surfaces; water-resistant solutions in wet spaces.
    • Tolerance to humidity changes in carpentry door/edge bands, compact laminate.

    Planning

    • Scaffolding/access plan: Safe access and logistics route on sloped plots.
    • Seasonal calendar: Pre-reservation and phased plan for summer season intensity.
    • Maintenance: Facade washing, joint renewal and periodic inspection schedule.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do wind and elevation difference make application difficult?

    It is applied safely with correct scaffolding/access plan, anchorage and time plan.

    How is water leakage prevented around terraces and pools?

    Watertight details are established with drip/sill profiles, slope control and flexible mastic.

    Does sun cause fading on interior surfaces?

    Risk is reduced with UV-resistant paint/coating and correct shading.

    Does humidity change affect doors in carpentry?

    Expansion control is ensured with appropriate carrier, edge band and tolerance installation.

    Do you offer warranty and maintenance?

    We provide workmanship warranty; maintenance schedule and cleaning guidelines are given upon delivery.
