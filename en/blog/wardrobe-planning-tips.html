    Introduction

    A well-planned walk-in closet or wardrobe maximizes storage capacity while maintaining accessibility and visual order. Proper zoning, hanging heights, drawer systems and lighting create a functional space.

    Zoning & Layout

    • Top zone (200‚Äì220 cm): Seasonal items, rarely used clothes, suitcases.
    • Middle zone (hanging): Shirts, jackets, dresses; rod height 160‚Äì180 cm from floor.
    • Lower zone (drawers/stacking): Underwear, socks, folded items, accessories.
    • Full-height hanging: Long dresses, coats; minimum 170 cm height.
    • Double hanging: Short items (shirts, pants) stacked; saves space.

    Hanging & Stacking Zones

    1. Hanging rod depth: Standard 60 cm; if space allows, 65‚Äì70 cm prevents shoulder marks.
    2. Stacking shelves: Depth 35‚Äì40 cm, height 25‚Äì30 cm per shelf; adjustable partitions.
    3. Drawer heights: Top drawer 8‚Äì10 cm (accessories), middle 15‚Äì20 cm (underwear), bottom 25‚Äì30 cm (folded items).
    4. Corner solutions: Rotating carousel or diagonal shelves for corner utilization.

    Shoe Storage & Accessories

    • Shoe shelves: Height 15‚Äì20 cm per row; pull-out shelves or angled display.
    • Tie/belt rods: Vertical rods or pull-out organizers; height 30‚Äì40 cm.
    • Watch/jewelry: Soft-lined drawers with dividers; lockable for valuable items.
    • Bags: Dedicated shelves or hanging hooks; avoid crushing.

    Lighting

    LED strips inside shelves, motion-activated under-shelf lighting and ceiling spotlights provide uniform illumination. Warm white (2700‚Äì3000K) for comfortable selection; dimming option.

    Materials & Mechanisms

    • Compact laminate: Moisture-resistant, scratch-resistant; ideal for coastal climate.
    • Soft-close mechanisms: Prevents slamming, extends lifespan.
    • Adjustable partitions: Flexibility for changing storage needs.
    • Ventilation: Perforated back panels or gap between shelves and wall.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the minimum depth for a walk-in closet?

    Minimum 150 cm depth allows comfortable passage; 180‚Äì200 cm is ideal for full functionality.

    How many hanging rods can I fit in a standard wardrobe?

    One full-height rod (170 cm) or two stacked rods (80‚Äì90 cm each) for short items.

    Do I need ventilation in the wardrobe?

    Yes. Perforated back panels or gaps prevent moisture buildup and odors, especially in coastal climates.
