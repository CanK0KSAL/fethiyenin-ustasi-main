    Protection and Use of Your Personal Data

    1. GENERAL INFORMATION

    As Fethiye's Master, we place great importance on the privacy of all users who visit our website and use our services. This Privacy Policy has been prepared to inform you about how your personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected.

    This policy has been prepared in accordance with Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data ("KVKK") and related legislation. For detailed information, you can visit our Privacy Notice page.

    2. INFORMATION COLLECTED

    2.1. Automatically Collected Information

    When you visit our website, the following information may be automatically collected:

    • IP address
    • Browser type and version
    • Operating system information
    • Pages visited and visit duration
    • Referral website (if any)
    • Date and time information

    2.2. Information Collected From You

    The following information is collected when explicitly requested from you:

    • Name and surname
    • Phone number
    • Email address
    • Address information
    • Project details and service request

    3. COOKIES

    Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, analyze site performance, and provide you with better service.

    3.1. Types of Cookies Used

    • Essential Cookies: Cookies necessary for the basic functions of the website
    • Analytical Cookies: Cookies used to analyze website usage
    • Functional Cookies: Cookies used to remember user settings such as language preferences

    3.2. Cookie Management

    You can manage or disable cookies from your browser settings. However, disabling some cookies may cause some features of the website to not work.

    4. PURPOSES OF USING INFORMATION

    Collected information is used for the following purposes:

    • Fulfillment of your service requests
    • Provision of free inspection and price quotes
    • Management of customer relationships
    • Conducting communication and information activities
    • Analysis and improvement of website performance
    • Fulfillment of legal obligations
    • Security and fraud prevention

    5. SHARING OF INFORMATION

    Your personal information is not shared with third parties except for legal obligations. However, it may be shared with the following groups in a limited manner when necessary for the provision of our services:

    • Our technical infrastructure and hosting service providers
    • Our email and communication service providers
    • Authorized public institutions and organizations due to legal obligations
    • Authorized authorities due to legal processes

    6. DATA SECURITY

    The security of your personal data is our priority. We take the following technical and administrative measures to protect your data:

    • SSL/TLS encryption technology
    • Secure server infrastructure
    • Regular security updates
    • Protection against unauthorized access
    • Staff training and confidentiality commitments

    7. DATA RETENTION PERIOD

    Your personal data is retained for the period required by the processing purposes and in accordance with legal retention obligations. When the processing purpose ends or the retention period expires, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

    8. YOUR RIGHTS

    Under KVKK, you have the following rights:

    • To learn whether your personal data is being processed
    • To request information about your processed data
    • To request correction of your data
    • To request deletion of your data
    • To object to the transfer of your data to third parties
    • To learn the third parties to whom your data has been transferred

    To exercise your rights, you can send an email to info@fethiyeninustasi.com or contact us by phone at +90 506 022 29 00.

    9. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

    Our website may contain links to third-party websites. The privacy policies of these websites are outside our control. If you click on third-party sites, we recommend that you review the privacy policies of those sites.

    10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

    Our website is not designed for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If we discover that we have collected information from a child, we will immediately delete that information.

    11. CHANGES

    This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Important changes will be announced on our website. We recommend that you check this page regularly.
